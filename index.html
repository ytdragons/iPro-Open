THE RULES

Read before you play!

🎵
5 surprise genres
You have 5 mystery genre cards — each one unlocked by the host when it's time to play. Every player gets a unique randomised card for each round.
👂
Listen & tap
When a song on your card plays, tap that square to mark it off. You can play all 5 genre cards at once!
🏆
How to win
You need to complete 2 full lines to call BINGO — rows, columns or diagonals all count. First person to shout BINGO! wins.
⚠️
No cheating!
The host can verify your card. Any squares marked for songs that haven't been played yet = disqualified. Play fair!
